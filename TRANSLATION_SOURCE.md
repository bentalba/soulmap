## Additional Context for Translators

### Tone Guidelines:
- **Professional yet warm:** Balance expertise with approachability
- **Mystical but grounded:** Use cosmic/celestial language while staying practical
- **Empowering:** Focus on self-discovery and personal growth
- **Trustworthy:** Emphasize experience, credentials, and authenticity

### Key Terms to Maintain Consistency:
- **Birth Chart / Natal Chart:** The astrological chart calculated from exact birth time/date/location
- **Synastry:** The astrological compatibility between two people
- **Composite Chart:** The combined energy/chart of a relationship
- **Planetary Transits:** Current planetary movements and their influence
- **Cosmic Blueprint:** The unique astrological pattern/destiny of a person
- **Souls:** Refers to clients/people in a spiritual/respectful way

### Cultural Notes:
- The website serves a global, multilingual audience
- Arabic speakers are a primary target demographic
- Professional astrology services (not fortune-telling)
- Focus on empowerment and self-knowledge, not predictions
