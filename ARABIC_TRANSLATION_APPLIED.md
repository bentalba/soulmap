## Translation Strategy

This translation employs **saj'** (سجع) - Arabic rhymed prose, a classical technique used in the Quran and traditional texts to create a musical, memorable, and authoritative flow.

### Key Characteristics:
- **Tone**: Warm (دافئ), empowering (متمكن), and mystical (صوفي/روحي)
- **Style**: Poetic yet professional, using terms familiar in spiritual contexts
- **Cultural Resonance**: Deep spiritual terminology like "روح" (soul), "كون" (cosmos), "هداية" (guidance)

## Poetic Techniques Used

### Saj' (Rhymed Prose) Examples:
1. **"بين حكمة الأقدمين و بصيرة العصر الحديث"**
   - Balances "wisdom of ancients" with "insight of modern era"

2. **"إيضاح للمسار والغاية / استبصارٌ للشخصية والأعماق"**
   - Creates rhythmic pattern with paired concepts

3. **"كل خريطة تحكي قصة، وكل كوكب يكشف حقيقة"**
   - Parallel structure with internal rhyme (قصة/حقيقة)

### Cultural Depth:
- **"إرشاد الأرواح"** instead of "أرواح موجهة" (active vs passive)
- **"السهولة"** instead of "الوصولية" (positive vs negative connotation)
- **Maritime metaphors** maintained: "الإبحاق" (sailing), "ربّانة" (captain)
